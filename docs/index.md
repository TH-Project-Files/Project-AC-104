# Argus Centurion (AC-104)

> **104 Points of Visibility and Command for Enterprise AI Security**

**Argus Centurion** is an open-source, vendor-agnostic framework for defending the **entire IT stack** against AI-accelerated threats — machine-speed attack chains, hyper-personalized phishing, deepfake impersonation, polymorphic malware, and compromised autonomous agents.

AI risk cannot be mitigated at the application layer alone. AC-104 layers defense in depth across five domains — **Governance & People, Identity & Access, Networking, Endpoints, and Applications & Data** — securing both the conventional infrastructure that AI-equipped adversaries attack and the LLMs, RAG pipelines, and agents your organization deploys.
